The Best Movies According to Ikram

My Top 3 movies of all-time.

The Batman

The Batman is the best interpretation of the Caped Crusader that offers something for everyone. It has a compelling story, rich and complex characters, a talented cast, and impressive production values, all of which combine to create a truly memorable and rewarding tale of what it means to be a superhero.

American Psycho

American Psycho is one of the greatest horror films of all time; with its disturbingly dark comedy, psychotic breakdowns of society and an unnerving look at the obsessive thoughts of a serial killer.

This is the End

“This Is The End” is a comedy about the apocalypse, The Biblical end of days.
